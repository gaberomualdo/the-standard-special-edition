Coronavirus Special

The Coronavirus has taken the world by storm. Ever since December 2019, the outbreak has continuously disrupted society across the world. In February, the school started being impacted by the virus with numerous different trip cancellations, eventually leading to school closure from Mar. 13.

These are unprecedented times, and it's no doubt that school closure and distance learning have affected all of our lives immensely. Below is a timeline of events and articles from The Standard leading up to school closure.

ASL Closed From March 13, 2020

As journalists, we have a responsibility to go where the story is. Now out of school, the virus has affected not just the global community, but our ASL community specifically.
